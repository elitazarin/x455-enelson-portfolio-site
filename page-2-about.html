ALLOW ME TO INTRODUCE MYSELF!

Nice to meet you! I am Elita Nelson, an aspiring UI/UX Designer, a runner, a big fan of plants, an artist, and cat mom to the cause of all entropy (Minnie). While studying Computer Science in college, I heard a bit about UX, but there werenâ€™t many opportunities to learn more.

After graduating in 2020, I opted for a less technical role as a consultant for a health software company. I was excited to work directly with our end users. During my time there, I was able to practice empathy for the people who use our software as I guided them through trainings and go-lives. Where there were clear sticking points, I adapted my trainings to try to mitigate those issues.

I realized that while I enjoyed working with end users, I was missing a bit of the creativity and problem solving I was able to exercise in CS. I recalled my previous interest in UX/UI and decided to look further into it. It seemed like it would be a perfect fit. I would be able to combine my passions for aesthetics and creativity with my knack for empathy and problem solving to create delightful human-centered experiences. So, I enrolled in the Professional Program in UX Design at UC Berkeley Extension and here I am, trying to do just that.
